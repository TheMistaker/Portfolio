About & Approach

Design that starts
with listening.

My process begins not with a blank canvas, but with a question: what does this person actually need? I believe the best design decisions emerge from honest observation — not assumptions — and from understanding the gap between what users say they want and what they actually do.

I work at the intersection of systems thinking and visual craft. Every project I take on ends with a solution that feels obvious in hindsight, which means the hardest work happens long before any screen is drawn.

  • Research-led, not assumption-led
  • Systems thinking over isolated screens
  • Accessibility as a foundation, not an afterthought
